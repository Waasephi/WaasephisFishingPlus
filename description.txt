This is a simple content mod focused entirely on adding new content related to fishing.
This includes many new fish, quests, food, and more.

The main feature of this mod is the Filleting Table, which allows fish to have more uses through the entire game.
All you need is a fish, and a strong enough filleting knife, and you can turn fish into useful materials.

This mod also includes some new fishing rods, equipment, and armor to help you fish a bit easier.
